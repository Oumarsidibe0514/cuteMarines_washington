WASHINGTON STATE FOCUSED

Pacific Northwest Marine

Washington is home to tremendous and gorgeous marine life. More than 3,000 life forms called the Puget sound home. The The Nature Conservancy is an organization dedicated to preserving these precious life forms that are not only critical to the survival of other species, but they are part of what makes Puget Sound our home. They create assessments about the life forms and the waters to help organizations make a better impact on their mission. Preserving biodiversity in Puget Sound is one of s most Nature Conservancy essential values.

Puget Soundkeeper

Clean water is the most crucial aspect of keeping people healthy it's also the most critical aspect of preserving marine life. Puget Soundkeeper is an organization dedicated to keeping the waters of Puget Sound clean and giving everyone easy access to clean water. They accomplish this by not only going on raids to physically remove debris from the waters but also by taking legal actions and proposing new Water Acts. Roughly speaking, Puget Soundkeeper is committed to maintaining the waters of Puget Sound taste ever like wine, to every species that lives in and outside of it.
More Info Visit their Home page



Island County Marine Resource Committee

Education is always, has always, will always be the key. This is Island County's core values when it comes to keeping the marine life safe in Puget Sound. They believe that getting the community engaged in what is happening to our wildlife is one of the most effective ways to restore and keep our waters healthy. Island County accomplishes its commitment through research and concrete data, and with that, they convince our local leaders to take action.
More Info Visit their Home page



Orca Network

Orcas are one of our most precious marine species in Puget Sound. The waters they inhabit must be clean and bountiful of food; otherwise, they will slowly and surely die out. The Orca Network is one of many organizations working hard to raise awareness of the human footprint's negative impact on their natural habitat. They're dedicated to raising awareness to all Washingtonians and keeping the waters safe for our fellow whales, painting our oceans with life.

WORLD FOCUSED

Ocean Conservancy

They focus more on public educations and advocation on laws that limit human footprint in the oceans, more specifically on marine habitats. Their philosophy is that every one of us depends on the sea, from food, to keeping the planet functioning. There is no life without the oceans. Using groundbreaking technology, Ocean Conservancy hopes to save the life in the oceans using one of our most useful tools––science. They believe that with all of us put together, there is no problem we cannot solve.
More Info Visit their Home page


Surfrider Foundation

They focus on reducing the amount of pollution that comes from ocean beaches. Making sure there's less garbage being dumped in the oceans through the beaches. Making sure we pick up after ourselves is Surfrider Foundation's strongly held beliefs. Beaches are one of the top polluters in the world; their goal is to make sure the beaches are not only safe for people but also safe for marine life. They also participate and create campaigns to keep the coasts clean.
